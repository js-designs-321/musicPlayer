Indian Playback Singer
Sonu Nigam (born 30 July 1973) is an Indian playback singer, composer. He sings predominantly in Hindi and Kannada language films. He has also sung in English, Bengali, Odia, Manipuri, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Nepali, Maithili, Malayalam and various Indian languages. Nigam has also released Indian pop albums and acted in a number of Hindi films. He is also regarded as the "Lord of Chords".
